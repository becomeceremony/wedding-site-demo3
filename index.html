Our Story

We met in a way neither of us expected.
It started as something small, almost insignificant.
But something about it felt different from the beginning.
We talked for hours without noticing time passing.
Slowly, those conversations became daily routines.
Then those routines became something we looked forward to.
We learned each other’s habits, fears, and dreams.
We grew through school, stress, and change together.
There were moments we laughed until it hurt.
And moments where life felt heavy and uncertain.
But we never stopped showing up for each other.
Over time, it stopped feeling like “just dating.”
It started feeling like building a life together.
We supported each other’s goals and growth.
We became each other’s calm in chaos.
And each other’s motivation when things got hard.
Somewhere along the way, love became certainty.
Not loud or dramatic, but steady and real.
Now we are ready for the next chapter.
And we are excited to share this moment with you.
